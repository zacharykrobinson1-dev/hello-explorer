Earth

Earth is a rocky planet with liquid water on its surface and an atmosphere that supports life. It has seasons, weather, and a protective magnetic field. Earth is also the only known place in the universe where life exists.

Quick facts

  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • One day: About 24 hours
  • Moon: 1 natural satellite
